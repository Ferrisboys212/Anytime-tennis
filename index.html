About Us

Bryce (left) and Jordan are both USPTA certified coaches with over 10 years of experience in the tennis industry. They have worked at country clubs, academies, and commercial clubs across the country.

Both graduated from Ferris State University with degrees in Professional Tennis Management. As 4.5/5.0 level players, they are passionate about teaching and continuing to improve their own games.

They have extensive experience running adult classes, ALTA practices, junior programs, and specialize in private lessons.
